15-122 Term Project: Pruned

The Project

Using python, I was tasked with creating a working program that would be representative of the knowledge that I had gained through the class. I chose to make a game in which the player could control the growth of a tree to grow enough flowers to win the level and proceed.

The game had many features, some of which included:
  • Random wind generation
  • Level editor
  • Soundtrack
